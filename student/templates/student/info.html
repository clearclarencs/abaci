About Abaci

Abaci is a free tool to help those who struggle. Give them a voice with this free to use tool.

Why?

Why Abaci?

As a student I would notice a lot of students struggle in silence and the teacher wouldnt realise as the studentys were always too embarrased to reach out. Abaci is free, anonymous and easy allowing you to help your students to reach out.

FAQ's

What is Abaci ?

Abaci is a free to use tool to increase communication between the teacher and their students to gauge understanding of topics. This leads to better and more efficient learning.

How much is Abaci ?

Abaci is completely free for all features and is financed by people who genuinely care about education for all.

How do I use Abaci?

Abaci is simple to use! Just create a free teachers account, create a class, add your topics and share the class code to your student's.
